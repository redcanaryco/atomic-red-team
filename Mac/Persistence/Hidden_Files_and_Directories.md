## Hide files

### Input:

    mv filename .filename

### Input:

	(Requires Apple Dev Tools)
    setfile -a V filename

## Hide Directories

### Input:

    chflags hidden /secret/dir

### Unhide:

    chflags nohidden

## Show all Hidden

### Execute within terminal:

    defaults write com.apple.finder AppleShowAllFiles YES
